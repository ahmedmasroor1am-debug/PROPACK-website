Manufacturing capability

A plant built for both prototypes and millions.

6 automated lines

Corrugators, die-cutters, folder-gluers and litho presses running in parallel, monitored in real time.

In-house QC lab

Burst strength, ECT, print registration and food-contact compliance — checked, logged and traceable per batch.
