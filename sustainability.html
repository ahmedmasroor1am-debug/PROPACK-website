Four commitments

How we keep packaging in the loop.

01 — Sourcing

Responsibly-sourced fibre

Responsibly-sourced board with high recycled content, traceable from mill to finished carton.

02 — Materials

Recyclable mono-materials

Single-stream constructions, water-based inks and plastic-free tapes — so the whole pack goes in one bin, the paper bin.

03 — Production

Lower-impact manufacturing

Solar-assisted lines, closed-loop water and zero board to landfill. Every offcut is baled and returned to the mill.

04 — End of life

Designed to be recycled again

Right-sized to cut material and freight, and engineered to be repulped — kerbside recyclable, home-compostable options available.
